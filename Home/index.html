An Artificial Intelligence Test Maker

Testify is an application that makes tests and exams using generative AI. Customize difficulty, score values, and more catered to the content you provide.

Why Choose Testify?

Testify has been developed and designed in the 2025 Rosehack hackathon. Testify uses the latest OpenAI technology and has been specifically trained to make and develop tests from course material PDF files. Our goal is that you have the best user experience while also saving yourself time in making tests for practice and academic exams.
